## Recruitment

After completing Map X-4: "The Altar of Raze," she becomes temporarily unavailable; then, at the start of the next chapter, view the city event "Newfound Power."

## Class Change

Automatically when permanently recruited.

- HP: +2
- Str: +1
- Def: +1
- Spd: +3
- Skills: Astra, Blade
