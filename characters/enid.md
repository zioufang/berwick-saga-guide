## Recruitment

After completing Map 6: "On the Front Lines," view the city event "Staring Eyes."

## Class Change

Requires:
- Lv 15
- Magic 5 (a Liquid Magic item can boost Magic to meet this)
- View the "Magical Knight" city event with Perceval alive

The change occurs at the start of the next chapter, and can happen anywhere from the start of Chapter 8 to the start of Chapter 13.

- Spd: +1
- Mag: +2
- Skills: Focus Chant
- Weapons: Fire, Thunder
- Weapon LV: Fire (LV×7/10), Thunder (LV×7/20)
- Items: Pallas Leia

## Growth Rate

- HP: 15%
- Str: 10%
- Def: 3%
- Agl: 20%
- Mnd: 20%
