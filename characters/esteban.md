## Recruitment

- Happiness: 5+

View the city event "Arina's Wish" in Map 3, then have Esteban participate in Map 6: "On the Front Lines" without being captured. During Map 9: "Breaking Bridges," the castle gate must be opened and the subsequent event viewed. After completing Map 9, view the city event "A Man's Naïveté."
