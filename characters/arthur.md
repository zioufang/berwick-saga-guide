## Recruitment

- Happiness: 10+ (Method 1), 1+ (Method 2)

- Method 1: View the city event "Dishonor" after Map 1-2: "Fledgling Knights," then view the city event "Exile."
- Method 2: Automatic, at the start of Chapter 8.

## Class Change

Requires:
- Lv 10
- Sword 20
- S Shield 20
- M Shield 10
- Must have leveled up after being permanently recruited

- HP: +2
- Str: +3
- Def: +2
- Spd: +1
- Weapons: Spear
- Weapon LV: (LV×7/12)
