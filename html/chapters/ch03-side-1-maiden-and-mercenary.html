Notes on the Map

You start with Sherpa and Aegina just outside the enemy fort, and Aegina must make her way to Rosalie in the bottom-right side of the map. This map is hilarious easy - mostly because Sherpa can just solo it. The Brymranger is unapologetically overpowered for this situation, and you can simply take a stroll downhill, and have Sherpa take care of the chaff that dare to chase Aegina down, giving her a casual stroll out of the fortress and into the safe corner where her handmaiden awaits.

As funny as it would be to have Sherpa do a U-Turn and just beat this map for you, we should actually do what we're asked to do. As soon as Aegina goes to speak with Rosalie, Rosalie will leave the map and your originally deployed forces will reinforce into the map. You must now fight your way back to the fort that Aegina and Sherpa just escaped from and must now seize it. By now, a lot of the enemies in the fort would've pushed out to fight you, only to meet an early death. The puny number of reinforcements won't be doing the enemies any favor, either. By the time you get to the fort, it should be mostly empty, allowing you to casually clear it out and seize at your leisure.

There is a bag in the bottom-left corner of the map that can be investigated to find a Liquid Vitality. This is a statbooster that raises max HP. Statboosters are extremely rare in Berwick Saga. You are looking at one of the only two Liquid Vitalities in the game, and one of the six total statboosters in the entire game. Make sure to grab it before you leave the map!

With the low pressure of this map, you can toy around as much as you want - hell, set up for a capture on the boss if you want. Dude's got a longsword, might as well yoink it. Whenever you are done, seizing the fort will recruit Aegina permanently and Sherpa will become a mercenary that we can hire. Aegina will additionally receive her Prf Wind Orb - Pallas Riana. The Pallas Riana is a quadruple-hit wind orb, and recharges by 8 uses every chapter (very important distinction: CHAPTER, not map). Make sure to take full advantage of the recharging properties of this amazing Wind Orb and enjoy your new mage!
