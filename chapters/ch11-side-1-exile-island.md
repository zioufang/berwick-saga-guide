## Objective Checklist

- Have Reese reach the temple in 20 turns.
- Bounty Target: Morrow (hidden near your starting location)
- Completed the Bounty Board (assuming X-1 is done) and received a full repair on Gram and Perseid
- Lootable Item: Vanishstone (open the treasure chest on southern island)
- Crafting Material: Winch (destroy the left ballista)
- Secured Owen's permanent recruitment in Chapter 12 by making sure he has 5+ Happiness at the end of this map to unlock the town event "Boundary"
- Secured Faye's access to her Prf sword in the Finale by having her recruited and viewing the town event "Unforgettable" (this town event only spawns if a sidequest in both 6-M and 7-M are completed)

## Notes on the Map

To put your mind at ease immediately - don't worry about the time limit. 20 turns is such an incredibly generous turncount that you will have to somehow massively screw up to not make it.

Welcome to scenic Berwick Island - a lot smaller than you may expect, but it doesn't make the welcome party any less rowdy. Despite the operation taking place at night, the enemies know we're coming and this is no stealth operation. Two ballistae are erected at the far side of Berwick Island, and they will gladly rain death upon you all map long. The small size of the map means that if we had a flier, we can dive the ballistae and remove the threats early, drastically reducing the pressure of the map, but...

The problem is: Reese, in his infinite wisdom, has sent Larentia into the temple ahead of the army as a scout. We therefore cannot deploy Larentia on the map that can be trivialized by her in an ultimate twist of cruelty. We'll be eating ballista shots pretty much all map long, so make sure that your weaker units are kept out of range. As long as there are hostiles giving the ballistae vision, they can shoot at you from the fog.

The soldier starting next to us is carrying a bag with 3 Antitoxins inside. The bag itself is likely a much better prize than its contents, and if you are in need of more inventory slots, definitely make an effort to steal the bag off the soldier or capture him for it. You have likely been feeling the crunch of filling inventories for a good chunk of the game now, and seeing a Bag that you can get definitely feels like manna from heaven.

Beside this soldier is the bandit Morrow, hiding amidst the trees. **Morrow is on the Bounty List**. With Larentia not present, another unit with Watchful may be helpful to detect him - or you can just run into him and take him in as usual. He isn't too tricky compared to all the other bounty targets we've arrested so far, the only big problem is that he is inside the northern ballista's range, so you may tank a few shots from the ballista while trying to take him in - but don't let that discourage you from claiming your prize.

Next, the lighthouse is occupied by an enemy that you can clear out. Parking a unit on the lighthouse will give you a large amount of vision on the island. Annoyingly enough, this vision does not cover the left ballista that's been covering for the soldier and Morrow, but it will reveal the location of the eastern one - if you need the extra intel, go for the scout, but the vision here is surprisingly unhelpful in the grand scheme of things.

Short of making a suicidal swim directly upwards, the rest of your army must reach the temple by going the very long way. Advance east toward the shoals and ascend to the high ground. Watch for enemy ambushes along the way, this map is filled with enemies that have Hide, and being caught off guard by an enemy that can chip you down for ballistae is a recipe for disaster. There are also a few priests scattered around the map that will be striking you with Thunder magic, take them down first to avoid the damage ramping up fast.

The chest on the eastern edge of the island may be unlocked for a Vanishstone - and just in time. Right past the shoal tile connecting the two halves of Berwick Island are two conspicuous forest tiles - each contains an ambushing crossbow waiting to snipe you. One of the crossbows has a Chu-Ko-Nu, making even Arrowbane procs unreliable to come out unscathed. Activate the Vanishstone to sneak past, or detect and take down the ambush before making your ascent - do this fast. The eastern ballista is still hitting you the entire time.

The ascent uphill to take down the ballista will be somewhat hellish. Lead the charge with your Arrowbane units - Kramer, Elbert, or Percy can all do well not just against the ballista, but even more Overwatching rangers in ambush as you go uphill. Kramer deserves special mention for his access to Climber, allowing him to retain his dodge rate in case Arrowbane fails while you go uphill. Target the ballista first and let your army swarm in to take down the rest of the archers. Spend a few turns regrouping before surmounting the final plateau.

The final plateau contains two horse archers and a named Lance Cavalry Lyle. Lyle will drop a horse for us to use when he is defeated, but mounted hit-and-run will be your final obstacle. The initial western ballista is still alive and well, and cavalry units have a very annoying tendency to constantly canto back into the ballista's range to dissuade you from pushing them. The ballista is on the tile directly left of the temple, and it may be in your best interest to dive it as fast as you can. This ballistician that's haunted us for the whole map will drop the crafting material Winch on death.

After all we've been through, the boss of this map guarding the temple is the least threatening part. Vadrius has decent stats, but will easily fold to you in a fight. Bring Reese to the temple and the map will end.

Larentia will leave your party at the end of this map - don't panic, she's not gone for long. You must play the rest of Chapter 11 without her. Normally, this is a trivial matter, since there are no more maps and you can just advance to Chapter 12 to get her back. It does get a bit complicated if you happened to unlock any of the four recruitment paralogues at this stage, and if you choose to do them here, you will have to do those maps without your flier.

But while we're in town, let's turn in Morrow's Bounty, and...
